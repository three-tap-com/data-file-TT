About the Institute

Established in 2008, IIT Bhubaneswar sets its sights on seamlessly integrating education, research, and industry engagement. Offering a diverse spectrum of programs including B.Tech., B.Sc.-B.Ed, M.Tech., M.Tech. (Blended mode), Joint M.Sc.-Ph.D, MS by Research, and Ph.D., the institute stands equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and laboratories. Its faculty, deeply entrenched in various research and industrial projects, drive innovation forward. Collaborations with esteemed universities, research institutions, and industries bolster its academic eminence. Committed to global recognition through exceptional graduates and pioneering research, IIT Bhubaneswar nurtures a vibrant learning community emphasizing interdisciplinary collaboration, creativity, critical thinking, and entrepreneurship. At its core, the institute champions a culture of respect, innovation, and strong industry-academia partnerships.

Presently, the Institute boasts a total of 3230 students. Among them, 1,977 are enrolled in Bachelor of Technology and Dual Degree programs, spanning Civil Engineering, Computer Science & Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering, Engineering Physics, Mechanical Engineering, and Metallurgical & Materials Engineering. Additionally, there are 93 B.Sc.-B.Ed. students, 210 M.Sc students, 441 M.Tech  students, 579 Ph.D. students and 11 MS-R students. In the Academic Year 2024-25, the Institute offers a range of B. ·_         Ph.D. Programme

·_         Minor and Micro Specialization in addition to B.Tech. Programme

The credit-based curriculum for B. Tech. programme is spread over 8 semesters and includes summer internship, as well as research projects. For evaluation in a subject, the Institute follows a seven-point grading system with letter grades and the corresponding grade points per credit. The Cumulative Grade Point Average (CGPA) is computed at the end of each semester. The CGPA obtained by a student reflects his/her performance up to and including that semester.


I. CURRICULUM AND PEDAGOGY:

 At IITBhuneswar, education is meticulously crafted to immerse students in a comprehensive learning odyssey. The institution's pedagogical ethos is a fusion of rigorous technical training intertwined with hands-on laboratory exploration, fostering an ecosystem ripe for the cultivation of creativity, innovation, and scholarly excellence. Beyond the traditional confines of technical education, the curriculum unfurls a rich tapestry of subjects, encompassing General Studies, Current Affairs, Entrepreneurship, Small Business Management, Contemporary World Literature, and Moral Thinking. Diving deeper into the contemporary zeitgeist, specialized courses in cutting-edge domains such as Big Data Analytics, Cybersecurity, and Cloud Computing are seamlessly integrated, ensuring graduates are primed for the challenges of tomorrow's world. To broaden the horizons of learning, strategic alliances with prestigious platforms like the National Programme on Technology Enhanced Learning (NPTEL) and Massive Open Online Courses (MOOC) open doors to a plethora of video courses. Furthermore, a diverse array of elective courses empowers students to curate a bespoke academic trajectory tailored to their passions and ambitions.

Rules For Change Branch

_Starting from the Academic Session 2024-25, there is no provision to change academic branch for any of the B.Tech. programmes. However, there is a provision for a B.Tech. student to choose an M.Tech. Programme  within the concerned discipline after completion of 3rd year (sliding mode).  The modalities are similar to a 5-year B.Tech.-M.Tech. dual degree programme.

Faculty

At IIT Bhubaneswar, a dynamic faculty cohort drawn from diverse backgrounds fuels the institution's academic prowess. Presently, the faculty body comprises 213 members, including 31 Professors, 1 MGM Chair Professor, 6 Visiting Professors, 70 Associate Professors, and 105 Assistant Professors. Each faculty member brings a wealth of expertise, holding a Ph.D. from prestigious national or international institutions, with many having garnered invaluable postdoctoral research experience abroad. With a keen dedication to excellence, the faculty prioritizes delivering top-tier instruction, spearheading groundbreaking research endeavors, and driving technological advancements forward. Their endeavors have garnered acclaim, securing numerous research and consultancy projects from esteemed entities such as CSIR, DST, DBT, DRDO, ISRO, and DEIT. Furthermore, they actively contribute to scholarly discourse through prolific publications, robust participation in international conferences, workshops, and symposiums, and frequent chairing of academic sessions, both domestically and internationally.

Facilities

IIT Bhubaneswar currently operates from its permanent campus at Argul, Khurda. All classes take place in the state-of-the-art classrooms and laboratories located on campus. Moreover, students benefit from access to practical training facilities at institutions such as the Central Tool Room and Training Center, Central Institute of Plastics Engineering & Technology, and Institute of Minerals and Materials Technology. The academic campus features Wi-Fi connectivity, modern classrooms equipped with audio-visual aids, well-stocked laboratories, a comprehensive library, high-speed internet, and video conferencing facilities.

Student Life at Institute

Nestled in its sprawling green campus at Argul, adjacent to the majestic Barunei Hills, IIT Bhubaneswar offers an idyllic setting for the holistic development of its students. Here, academic excellence thrives through a comprehensive educational approach that seamlessly integrates curricular and extracurricular activities. The institute provides comfortable accommodation with essential amenities for both male and female students in its permanent campus hostels. These modern facilities include round-the-clock internet access, a fully-equipped gymnasium, inviting common rooms, and various recreational spaces. Moreover, students have access to 24-hour medical assistance, with emergency care available onsite and in nearby hospitals in Bhubaneswar city for specialized medical needs. Complementing these services is a dedicated counselling cell staffed by qualified Psychologist-Counsellors and student guides, offering invaluable support to students in their day-to-day lives. The CST includes 3 counsellors, 2 coordinators, and about 50 student guides.

The Counselling Service Team (CST) at IIT Bhubaneswar is dedicated to promoting the personal, academic, professional, and social growth of students, equipping them with skills to navigate life's challenges. The team provides a safe and confidential space for students to address their concerns and difficulties, including behavioural and clinical issues affecting their academic and personal lives. Since CST members are students themselves, they empathize with the challenges faced by newcomers. This mentorship, led by senior CST students under faculty guidance, fosters a supportive culture that strengthens interpersonal relationships among students. With 29 student guides assigned to mentor groups of 8-10 first-year students, personalized support and guidance are readily available.

Furthermore, IIT Bhubaneswar prioritizes the welfare of its female members through the Women's Welfare Council. This council is committed to creating a safe and inclusive environment that empowers women and addresses their specific needs and concerns. It organizes various initiatives and programs aimed at promoting gender equality, enhancing women's participation in academic and extracurricular activities, and providing support and guidance on issues related to health, safety, and well-being. Through its efforts, the Women's Welfare Council plays a vital role in fostering a culture of respect, equality, and empowerment within the institute community.

Financial Assistance

Scholarships / Financial Assistance Provided by the Institute:   The Institute provides Remission of Tuition Fees to eligible students, with criteria based on parental gross annual income. Students whose parental income does not exceed Rs. 5 lakh per annum are eligible. Those with parental income below one lakh per annum qualify for Full Remission of Tuition Fees, while those with incomes between one lakh and five lakhs per annum are eligible for 2/3rd Remission of Tuition Fees.

Govt. of India Scholarships (Provided by Govt. of India) : The Institute endorses various Government of India Scholarship Schemes, including the Central Sector Scholarship Scheme for "Top Class Education for Schedule Caste (SC) Students," the Central Sector Scholarship Scheme for "Top Class Education for Schedule Tribe (ST) Students," Scholarships for Students with Physical Disabilities, Post-Matric Scholarships, and the Ishan Uday Scholarship, among others.

State Scholarships (provided by different states): The Institute also supports Scholarship Schemes offered by various State Governments through their respective scholarship portals. Examples include the Kalia Scholarship (Government of Odisha), E-Medhabruti Scholarship (Government of Odisha), Mukhya Mantri Medhawi Vidyarthi Yojana Scholarship (Government of Madhya Pradesh), and MAHA-DBT Scholarship (Government of Maharashtra), among others.

 PM-Vidyalaxmi Education Loan: The Institute supports the PM-Vidyalaxmi Scheme to provide education loans to meritorious students who get admission in the top quality higher educational institutions of the nation so that financial constraints do not prevent any youth of India from pursuing quality higher education.

 

Training & Placement

The Career Development Cell (CDC) at IIT Bhubaneswar is dedicated to empowering students to explore, define, and achieve their career aspirations. It offers personalized one-on-one counselling, continuous consultations throughout the career-planning journey, and support in setting and attaining goals through various career exploration activities. The overarching goal is to equip students with lifelong skills and tools necessary for professional growth, successful job searches, and overall career fulfilment. By guiding students in shaping and managing their career paths, the CDC aims to instill the essential elements needed for becoming well-rounded and successful professionals.

In previous placement seasons, IIT Bhubaneswar has achieved remarkable success. In the 2021-22 season, 94.77% of B.Tech students secured placements in top organizations and Multi-National Companies (MNCs).In the 2022-23 placements, approximately 93.91% of B.Tech students received offers from reputable organizations. Similarly, in the 2023-24 placements, approximately 86.07% of B.Tech students received offers from reputable organizations

Despite challenges faced during the 2024-25 placement season, the CDC remains committed to ensuring the success of its students. While the global market slowdown and hiring freeze posed obstacles, the CDC continues to work tirelessly to secure promising job opportunities for students. As of now, close to 78% of B.Tech students have received job offers, with the placement season ongoing until the end of July 2025.

Placement participation has been strong this year, with active involvement from leading Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs) such as Bharat Electronics Limited (BEL), Engineers India Limited (EIL), Bharat Petroleum Corporation Limited (BPCL), Hindustan Petroleum Corporation Limited (HPCL), Oil India Limited (OIL), and MECON Limited. In addition, top Multi-National Companies (MNCs) like Google, Microsoft, Reliance Industries Ltd, Flipkart, D.E. Shaw, Oracle, Unistring Tech Solutions, Accenture Japan, Media.Net, Jaguar Land Rover, Analog Devices, Zomato, MathWorks, Caterpillar, HCL Technologies, Tata Steel R&T, AIRA Matrix, ARM (Advanced RISC Machine), Maruti Suzuki, Applied Materials, L&T Limited, Jindal Stainless Limited, Jindal Steel & Power, Vedanta, Turing, Tiger Analytics, and Tata Steel have also taken part in the placement drive. Moreover, pre-final year B.Tech students have successfully secured internship opportunities with several prestigious organizations.

Recreational/Extra Curricular activities

IIT Bhubaneswar offers a myriad of opportunities for students to immerse themselves in a rich tapestry of co-curricular and extracurricular pursuits. Sports, Yoga, NSS and NCC are seamlessly integrated into the B.Tech. curriculum's Extra Academic Activity (EAA) component in the first year not only help to develop physical and mental wellness but also provide the students the opportunity to develop interpersonal skills. Extra Academic Activities guided by dedicated Faculty Advisors and faculty mentors and spanning robotics, idea pools, code marathons, finance and business managements athletics, sports, filmmaking, photography, music, dramatics and dance. These activities epitomize the institute's dedication to holistic education.

The campus boasts state-of-the-art facilities, including a gymnasium, cricket grounds, football ground, basketball, badminton, volleyball, squash, table tennis and lawn tennis courts, fostering a vibrant culture of physical fitness and sportsmanship. Dedicated coaching classes are provided in several popular sports by well-trained sports coaches. Each year, students showcase their prowess at the Inter-IIT Sports Meet, Inter IIT Technical Meets, and Inter IIT Cultural Meets in addition to several regional and national sports, technical and cultural competitions, embodying the spirit of competitiveness and camaraderie.

 At the heart of student life lies the Students' Gymkhana, the pulsating hub for all activities at IIT Bhubaneswar. Here, the flagship event—Pravaah, the Annual Cultural and Technopreneurship festival of IIT Bhubaneswar—unleashes waves of creativity, innovation, and cultural vibrancy. Pravaah, a pinnacle of techno-management excellence, ignites the flames of innovation and entrepreneurship through workshops, competitions, and performances that draw participants from across the nation. It is a grand celebration of socio-cultural heritage, captivates audiences with its diverse array of events, competitions, and workshops, channeling youthful energy into positive social impact. As beacon of entrepreneurship and leadership, it propels students into the realms of industry and innovation, empowering them to become the pioneers of tomorrow. It catalyses a culture of entrepreneurial spirit, inspiring students to venture fearlessly into the world of startups and innovation.

 Ashwamedha, the annual sports fest of IIT Bhubaneswar, brings sportspersons from all over India to compete for the coveted medal in many sports.

 Additionally, the General Championship is the mega annual Inter-departmental/school Techno-cultural-sports Championship of the institute where students from all department participate in large numbers, fostering a spirit of teamwork, competition, and excellence among students.
