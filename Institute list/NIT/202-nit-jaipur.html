About the Institute

Institute History:
MNIT Jaipur was established in 1963 as a joint venture of the Government of India and the Government of Rajasthan with the name Malaviya Regional Engineering College, Jaipur. Subsequently; on June 26, 2002 the college has been given the status of National Institute of Technology and on August 15, 2007, proclaimed Institute of National Importance through Act of Parliament. The Institute is fully funded by Ministry of Education (MoE), Government of India. More than 12,000 students have graduated from the Institute, since its establishment.

Campus Introduction:
Extending over an area of about 317 acres of lush greenery, the campus of the Institute is imaginatively laid-out with a picturesque landscape. It presents a spectacle of harmony in modern architecture, and natural beauty which enthralls and inspires. The campus of the institute consists of the institute buildings, halls of residence and staff colony. It is a residential campus offering accommodation to faculty, staff and students.
The campus provides all essential amenities for community living like staff clubs, primary health center, banks, post office, community centre, school, staff residences, gymnasium, playing fields, guest houses, 24 Hours internet connectivity, and canteen etc.

Academic Structure

MNIT Jaipur has recently revised the teaching schemes of various UG programs. The salient points of UG Program at MNIT Jaipur are
  1. Curriculum updated (in 2022) as per NEP guidelines
  2. Possibility to complete minor specialization offered by other department
  3. Possibility to earn Honors in own department
  4. Students can opt for semester long/year long internship
  5. Some credits can be earned from selected online platforms 
  6. Direct admission to PhD program of reputed IITs

Rules For Change Branch

MNIT Jaipur does not allow change of branch of the students and the students have to continue with the same branch/stream allotted by JOSAA (Joint Seat Allocation Authority)/CSAB (Central Seat Allocation Board).
