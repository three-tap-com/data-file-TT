About the Institute

Maulana Azad National Institute of Technology (MANIT) Bhopal: A Legacy of Excellence in Technical Education

Established in 1960 as one of the eight pioneering Regional Engineering Colleges (RECs), Maulana Azad National Institute of Technology (MANIT) Bhopal stands as a premier institution dedicated to advancing technical education in India. Recognized as an Institute of National Importance, MANIT carries forward the legacy of its namesake, Maulana Abul Kalam Azad, the first Education Minister of independent India and a distinguished scholar.

Originally founded as the Maulana Azad College of Technology (MACT) through a collaborative initiative between the Government of India and the Government of Madhya Pradesh, the institute was conceived to nurture exceptional engineering talent from across the nation. A significant milestone was achieved on June 26, 2002, when the Ministry of Human Resource Development (now Ministry of Education), Government of India, elevated its status to that of a National Institute of Technology (NIT). MANIT proudly celebrated its Golden Jubilee in 2010 and its Diamond Jubilee in 2020, commemorating decades of contribution to technological progress and nation-building.

Campus and Location: Strategically situated in the heart of Bhopal, the capital city of Madhya Pradesh, MANIT boasts a sprawling, self-contained 650-acre campus. This prime location ensures excellent connectivity via rail, road, and air, facilitating easy access for students and visitors nationwide.

Academic Distinction: MANIT is distinguished by its highly qualified faculty, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and an unwavering commitment to achieving the highest standards of technical excellence. The Institute fosters a vibrant academic environment through internationally relevant curricula, innovative pedagogical approaches, and a holistic focus on student development. It is renowned for its significant contributions to Research and Development (R&D), robust industrial consultancy, and impactful continuing education and training programs for both academia and industry professionals.

Comprehensive Academic Programs: MANIT offers a diverse spectrum of academic programs through its well-established departments and advanced research centers, supported by approximately 100 specialized laboratories.

State-of-the-Art Facilities: The campus is equipped with comprehensive infrastructure, including dedicated departmental buildings, a central library, a modern Computer Centre, an Energy Centre, specialized workshops, multiple guest houses, extensive athletic grounds, a Sports Complex with gymnasium, a Student Activity Centre, and an Open Air Auditorium. Essential amenities such as healthcare services, Post Office, banking facilities (including ATM), and campus-wide Wi-Fi ensure a conducive living and learning environment.

Innovation and Entrepreneurship: MANIT actively cultivates an entrepreneurial spirit. The MANIT Rolta Incubation Centre, established with philanthropic support from alumnus Shri K. K. Singh, provides aspiring innovators with the necessary infrastructure, mentorship, and resources to transform ideas into viable commercial products and services.

Residential Life and Student Activities: As a fully residential institute accommodating nearly 6000 undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral students, MANIT offers a vibrant campus life. Twelve well-maintained hostels provide comfortable accommodation. A dynamic student community engages in diverse co-curricular pursuits through over 15 active clubs. Flagship annual events, including the national-level technical festival 'Technosearch' and the cultural extravaganza 'Maffick', serve as platforms for students nationwide to showcase their technical prowess and creative talents.

Fee Structure

Tuition fee for students taking admission to B. Tech, B.Arch and B. Plan courses is  Rs 1,25,000/- per student per annum from the academic year 2016 - 2017. (vide MHRD order F .No.33 - 4/ 2014 - TS.III, dated 24th June, 2016) detailed fee structure is available on institute website-www.manit.ac.in

The tuition fee is subject to the following for protecting the interests of the socially and economically backward students:- 

(a) The SC/ ST/ PwD students shall get complete fee waiver. 

(b) The most economically backward students (whose family income is less than Rs 1.0 lakh per annum) shall get full remission of the fee. 

(c) The other economically backward students (whose family income is between Rs 1.0 lakh to 5.00 lakh per annum) shall get remission of 2/3rd of the fee. (Rs. 41668.00 per year and Rs. 20834.00 per semester)

(d) Tuition Fee - Family income more than Rs. 5 laks Rs. 125000.00 per year and Rs. 62500.00 per semester.

       (iii) Food/Mess fee approx. Rs. 30000 per year.

MANIT Bhopal: Campus, Infrastructure, and Facilities

Prime Location and Expansive Campus: Strategically situated in Bhopal, the capital of Madhya Pradesh and renowned as the 'City of Lakes,' Maulana Azad National Institute of Technology (MANIT) resides on an expansive and verdant 650-acre campus. State-of-the-Art Academic and Research Infrastructure:

  • Central Library: Serving as a vital knowledge resource, the Central Library holds an extensive collection exceeding 150,000 books and bound technical journals. It subscribes to over 90 national and international print journals and provides access to a vast array of e-journals and digital resources. A dedicated Book Bank facilitates long-term loans of textbooks to support economically weaker students.
    • Details: http://www.manit.ac.in/about-central-library

  • Central Computing Facility (CCF): MANIT boasts a cutting-edge CCF equipped with High-Performance Computing (HPC) clusters and advanced Virtualization capabilities. The HPC facility provides substantial computational power (measured in TFLOPS) for demanding scientific research, data processing, and complex calculations. The Virtualization cluster enhances server deployment and web service delivery for institutional needs.
    • Details: http://www.manit.ac.in/content/about-ccf

  • Core Facilities: Supporting diverse academic programs are dedicated departmental buildings, specialized workshops, the Energy Centre, and other facilities crucial for practical learning and research.

Training & Placement

         MANIT Bhopal places a strong emphasis on the career development of its students through a dedicated Training and Placement Cell. This cell serves as a crucial link between students and the industry, facilitating various activities aimed at preparing students for the professional world and connecting them with potential employers.  

         Key Activities and Functions of the Training and Placement Cell:

  •  Industry Interaction: The cell actively engages with a wide range of companies from diverse sectors, including IT, core engineering, consulting, finance, and more. This involves inviting companies for campus recruitment drives, coordinating industry interactions, guest lectures, and workshops. 

  •  Placement Drives: A primary function is organizing and conducting campus placement drives throughout the year. This includes managing the logistics of company visits, conducting pre-placement talks, written tests, group discussions, and interviews.   

  •   Internship Assistance: The cell facilitates internship opportunities for students, which are considered valuable for gaining practical experience and often lead to Pre-Placement Offers (PPOs). They assist students in finding relevant internships and guide them through the application process.

  •   Training and Preparation: The Training and Placement Cell, sometimes in collaboration with other departments or external trainers, organizes training programs to enhance students' soft skills, communication abilities, interview techniques, and technical knowledge relevant to the recruitment process.  
  •           
  •   Career Counseling: The cell provides guidance and counseling to students regarding career options, resume building, and interview preparation. 
  •          
  •   Maintaining Placement Statistics: The cell maintains records of placement statistics, including the number of students placed, the companies that recruited, and the salary packages offered.

           Placement Overview and Statistics:

           MANIT Bhopal has a commendable placement record, attracting a significant number of recruiters annually. While statistics can vary year to year and across branches, here's a general overview based on recent data:

  •            Participating Companies: Over 200 companies reportedly visit the campus for recruitment each year.

  •            Placement Rates: Placement rates vary by department, with some branches like Computer Science and Engineering often reporting high   placement percentages (e.g., over 90% in some recent years). Other branches also show strong placement numbers, though percentages may differ.

  •            Salary Packages:
    •            Highest Package: The highest salary packages offered have reached significant figures, with reported highs going up to INR 82 LPA or  even higher in recent placement drives, particularly in the IT and CSE sectors. 

    •            Average Package: The average salary packages also show positive trends, with overall averages for B.Tech programs often reported in the   range of INR 11 LPA to INR 16 LPA in recent years. Branch-wise averages can vary, with CSE typically having higher averages (e.g., INR 19 LPA to INR 25 LPA).

    •            Median Package: Median salary figures provide another perspective on typical compensation. Recent median packages for B. Tech. students have been reported around INR 11 LPA to INR 13.8 LPA.

  •            Placement Trends: The institute has generally shown an increasing trend in the number of students placed and the average/median salary   packages over the past few years.

           Specific Policies:

           MANIT Bhopal's Training and Placement Cell operates under certain policies to ensure a structured and fair placement process. These may   include guidelines regarding student eligibility for placements, the "One Student One Job" policy (where a student is generally allowed to accept only one job offer), and specific rules regarding pre-placement offers and internships.   

           Overall, the Training and Placement activities at MANIT Bhopal are comprehensive, aiming to equip students with the necessary skills and     connect them with suitable career opportunities in the industry. The consistently good placement statistics and the diversity of recruiting companies reflect the effectiveness of the cell's efforts and the quality of education and talent at the institute.

Location and Accessibility

Strategic Location and Excellent Accessibility of MANIT Bhopal

          Maulana Azad National Institute of Technology (MANIT) Bhopal is strategically located in the vibrant heart of Bhopal, the picturesque "City of Lakes," in the state of Madhya Pradesh. The institute boasts a sprawling and verdant campus spanning 650 acres, providing a conducive and serene environment for academic and extracurricular pursuits while being seamlessly integrated with urban conveniences.

          Bhopal, as the capital of Madhya Pradesh, benefits from excellent connectivity to all major parts of India via a well-developed network of rail, road, and air transportation.

          Accessibility:

  • Air: Raja Bhoj Airport (BHO) is situated approximately 18 kilometers from the MANIT campus, ensuring convenient air travel access.

  • Rail: Bhopal is served by two main railway stations: Bhopal Junction and Habibganj (now Rani Kamalapati) Railway Station. MANIT Bhopal is conveniently located approximately 9 kilometers from Bhopal Junction and a mere 6 kilometers from Rani Kamalapati Railway Station.

  • Road: The city's robust road network provides excellent connectivity to the rest of the country. The Inter State Bus Terminal (ISBT) Bhopal is located around 7 kilometers from the institute, facilitating easy bus travel.

          The climate in Bhopal is generally moderate, with temperatures typically ranging from 10°C to 45°C across the different seasons.
