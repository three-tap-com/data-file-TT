About the Institute

     National Institute of Technology Andhra Pradesh is the 31st institution among the chain of NITs started by the Government of India. NIT Andhra Pradesh is established in the state of Andhra Pradesh in the academic year 2015-2016. Currently, the Institute is offering B.Tech., M.Tech., Ph.D. and MS programmes.

   A new campus has been established with 172.6 acres of land adjacent to Chennai-Kolkata Highway (NH-16) in the air-strip lands of Tadepalligudem. CPWD had taken up the construction activity for all the buildings using Prefabrication construction technology. The construction work of Phase 1A buildings (Academic & laboratory complexes, Hostels, faculty and staff quarters etc.) and 1B (additional academic & Laboratory infrastructure along with full-fledged recreation & students amenities centre)  is already completed. Presently the institute is fully running from the permanent campus. 

   The goal of the  Institute is to provide aesthetically pleasing environment-friendly campus facilities that enhance the learning, teaching and working atmosphere for the stake holders of the Institute as well as an inviting and pleasant experience for the visitors.

   NIT Andhra Pradesh has recruited 100 young Adhoc faculties to supplement and strengthen the teaching process apart from 50 regular faculties. Most of the faculties are doctorates and with excellent academic and research profile. The Institute is providing good academic ambience for teaching-learning process. The Institute has recruited 68 regular non-teaching staffs.

   The Institute has adopted a mission and vision policy to become a leader in providing best technical education among the recently established Institutions. The Institute has also taken leap in R&D activities in the all the core and applied areas of engineering, science and interdisciplinary researches with emphasis on innovation and incubation of technologies. MOUs with several new industries and institutions have already been signed to execute collaborative academic and research activities.

    The Training and Placement Section is rigorously trying for placing maximum number of students. More than 90% undergraduate students were placed in the last three years, with average package of >8 LPA and maximum package >47 LPA.

    

Vision of the Institute:

     To nurture and produce highly competent engineers, scientists and entrepreneurs committed towards catering to futuristic societal challenges through holistic education synergetic with innovations and vibrant research eco-system.

Mission of the Institute:

  1. To implement best practices in teaching-learning methodologies for establishing dynamic knowledge-connected society.
  2. To create a conducive environment for carrying out research in multi-disciplinary areas and thereby nurturing novel thinking capabilities.
  3. To strengthen industry-institute interface to inculcate entrepreneurship abilities.
  4. To address all technological needs of the Nation for self-sustenance.

About Training & Placement cell NIT Andhra Pradesh

Mission:

“"Prepare Quality Engineers for Successful Future”

Vision:

“To be a nationally recognized organization that attracts, rewards, retains outstanding faculty, students and staff."

Training and Placement Section is the marketing division of the institute. This section, acting as an interface between the college and companies, has maintained symbiotic and purposeful relationship with industries across the country.

As a result, it is willing to build up an impressive record both in terms of percentage of students placed as well as number of companies visiting the campus. The section hosts companies o campus and ensures that every aspirant is assured of a bright career of his/her choice.

Activities

1. Nurtures industry-institute interaction, by organizing and coordinating frequent industrial visits, inplant training and projects of industrial relevance for the students, with the sole aim o zeroing down the hiatus between the Industry and the academia.

2. The cell has in its active file a database of nearly 500 companies.

3. Helps every student to define his/her career interest through individual expert counseling.

4. Makes available updated data base and job profile of the companies and thus helps each student to analyze and choose a company of his/her interest.

5. Organizes and coordinates campus placement programs, to fulfill its commitment of a career of every aspirant.

6. Receives and forwards the feedback from the visiting companies pertinent to curriculum improvement to ensure that our students are industry ready.

Location and Accessibility

Tadepalligudem is well connected by direct train services to most major cities of the country like Hyderabad, Bangalore, Chennai, Vijayawada, Visakhapatnam, Kolkata. Tadepalligudem is the nearest railway station to the Institute on Vijayawada – Visakhapatnam main line. The new campus of the Institute is about 10 minutes’ drive (3 km) from the Tadepalligudem railway station. Private taxi or auto rickshaw can be hired to reach the Institute. 

Tadepalligudem is also well connected to Rajahmundry (45 kms), Vijayawada (110 kms) and Visakhapatnam (240 kms) by road transport. The nearest airports to Tadepalligudem are situated at Rajamundry, Vijayawada and Visakhapatnam. The nearest international airport to Tadepalligudem is the Rajiv Gandhi International Airport (HYD), Hyderabad (400 kms from Tadepalligudem).
