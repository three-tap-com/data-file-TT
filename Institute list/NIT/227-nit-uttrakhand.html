About the Institute

With rapid advancements in Science and Technology, the contemporary times demand immense contribution from young skillful minds, with a vision and aim to contribute towards efficient yet sustainable development. National Institute of Technology Uttarakhand- one of the thirty-one Institutes of National Importance in the country, abides by the afore-mentioned principle by constantly providing an encouraging environment for education and training of technical professionals as well as grooming the skills of the students through impeccable learning courses.

Established in 2009 under the Act of Parliament by the Ministry of Education (Shiksha Mantralaya), NITUK is the Technical Education Hub in the Green and Clean lap of the Himalayas, precisely located in the Land of the Gods (known as Dev Bhoomi), the state of Uttarakhand. With a peaceful and serene atmosphere that environs the institute, the students are motivated to achieve academic and technical excellence in the streams of their choice. It is noteworthy that NITUK is located on the banks of Alaknanda River in Srinagar (Garhwal), about 100 KM ahead of Haridwar and Rishikesh, two of the holiest pilgrimages in Sanatan Dharma and also known as the gateways to the “Chhota Char Dham”. The impeccable geographical location boosts leaning and training as Haridwar, Rishikesh, and the mountains beyond have been the choice of Rishis and Munis since time immemorial and are the originators of rich old spiritual and intellectual establishment of glorious India [Capital of ancient Indian wisdom and university (known as Gurukuls)]. The Institute admits students to B. Tech. in Civil Engineering, Computer Science and Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering, Electrical and Electronics Engineering, and Mechanical Engineering through JEE Mains (formerly known as AIEEE) and to M. Tech. in all Engineering programmes through GATE, both the examinations conducted all over India by NTA. The Institute also offers Ph. D. in the streams of Engineering, Sciences, and Humanities. The peaceful, green and clean environment endows natural advantage to the institution and is greatly reflected in its excellent teaching- learning ambience, young and dedicated faculty, and significant contribution to research work and commitment to the community.

 NITUK has initiated the implementation of the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020 for the benefit of the students, community and the nation as a whole. We are dedicated to working on interdisciplinary research and development initiatives in emerging technologies such as Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Renewable Energy, Electric vehicles, etc. Contributing to the Hon’ble Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi’s initiatives on “Atmanirbhar Bharat”, we focus on the initiatives of the Career-related Counselling from time to time for the students shortly in order to help them emerge not only as great technocrats, but also job creators for others in the country and overseas. The institute envisions- To provide global impetus to education and innovation for the sustainable development of industry and society.

“उद्योग और समाज के सतत विकास के लिए शिक्षा और नवाचार को वैश्विक महत्व प्रदान करना”

For an impactful achievement of the afore-mentioned aim, we at NITUK are focusing upon innovation, research, latest technologies, industry outset, and six monthly/one year industry internships. Several MOUs have been signed with top-notch Academic Institutions/universities in India and Abroad, Government Research organizations, and Industries for the research collaborations. NITUK faculty and researchers are leading in cutting-edge research. They have acquired sponsored research projects from research funding agencies such as DST, MHRD, MeitY, UCOST, UGC-DAE CSR, etc. With an increase in funding, research publications, graduate student productivity, and industry outreach activities, we are marching ahead to enhance our research productivity. We are delighted to express that in the current placement session, about 49% students have already been placed in very reputed and core companies. Since its inception, this Institute has grown rapidly and tremendously. It has established itself as one of the best technical educational institutes across the country with 131st  rank in NIRF India ranking 2022 and  in band 101-150 ranking in NIRF India ranking 2023 in the Engineering Category among almost 12,000 Institutions in the country, despite its remote geographical location and limited resources available in the mountainous region.

Furthermore, NITUK is recognized in the band "PROMISING" under the category "Institute of National Importance & Central Universities/CFTs (Technical)" in the Atal Ranking of Institutions on Innovation Achievement (ARIIA) 2021. Institute has its own rafts, and our students are accustomed to enjoying rafting on the rivers that ornament the beauty of the region. During winters, there are many attractive and adventure spot around the Institute, including Tehri Dam, Dhanaulti, Auli, Mussoorie, Chopta, and Khirshu for snowfall and tracking. Yamunotri, Gangotri, Kedarnath, and Badrinath, the four holiest pilgrimages sites (known as Char Dham Yatra), are located about 150 KM ahead of the Institute. You are welcome to become our partners/stockholders in progress and undertake this exciting journey in the future.

We welcome you to NITUK, to be a part of it and make it one of the best academic institutions located in the country.

Rules For Change Branch

Program change at the end of first year (UG Only).

The following regulations apply for change of program at the end of first year, i.e. end of the 2nd semester. 

(a) A student is eligible to apply for change of discipline at the end of first year only, provided he/she satisfies the following criteria:

 i. CGPA: = 7.50

ii. Earned credits at the end of first academic session ≥ 40 

(b) Change of the discipline will be permitted strictly in the order of merit as determined by their CGPA at the end of first year subject to the limitation that the actual number of students in the third semester in the discipline to which the transfer is to be made, should not exceed the sanctioned strength. 

(c) For a student with CGPA 9.5 or more, even if a vacancy does not exist, he/she will be permitted to change provided the strength in the discipline to which the change is being sought does not exceed by 5% of the approved strength.

Facilities

Medical Facility – The Institute has a dispensary that provides complete free basic medical facilities/services for the students of the Institute for 24 x 7 and 7 days. In the Dispensary Section, four nursing staff members are available throughout the day, and a visiting doctor from the Government hospital (Srikot) offers daily service during the evening (6 pm to 7 pm). Institute Ambulance is also provided to students 24 hours a day in case of an emergency.

Gymnasium - The Institute has a Gymnasium equipped with the necessary facilities to maintain students' health.

Power Back-up - The whole campus is under complete power back-up (4×30 kVA DG sets).

Internet Facility - The campus has a Wi-Fi facility and a high-speed internet connection.

Institute Mess—Special care is taken to provide a clean and hygienic environment in the Mess's premises. Food quality and water purity are checked regularly by concerned authorities.

Conference Hall—The Institute has a Conference Hall with a capacity of 70 people. It is used for conducting meetings, seminars, workshops, etc.

Book Bank Facility - The Library houses a total collection of over 33,500 printed books (with 6000 titles), which includes Text Books and Reference Books on different subjects.

ATM - An SBI ATM is available in the Polytechnic campus of the Institute.

Canteen - The canteen of the Institute serves cafeteria items at a reasonable price.

Auditorium—The Institute has a Multipurpose auditorium with a capacity of 300 persons, which is used for seminars, workshops, movie screenings, and more.

Open Air Theater—An open-air theatre was established on the ITI campus of the Institute, which hosted various major Cliffesto events.

Student Life at Institute

The Institute provides a homely environment for students. It has adopted the principle of "Homes away from Home." Hostels have been provided for the students with all the necessary facilities. The Institute provides accommodation for more than 800 students on the campus. Wi-Fi is provided in the hostel for Internet access. There is a separate hostel for girls. A matron/Nurse stays in the girls’ hostel for 24 hours.

There are various indoor and outdoor sports facilities, such as Volleyball, Badminton, Basketball, Football, Cricket, Table Tennis, Chess, Carom, a gymnasium, and many more. The sports arena has well-equipped facilities so that the students can practice at night. The Institute conducts adventurous sports activities like ice skiing at Auli and rafting. Sports meet, and departmental and institutional gatherings are the mega sports festivals in our institute.

Alumni Affairs

The office of Alumni Affairs at NIT, Uttarakhand (NITUK) provide a forum that encourage and promote interaction and networking among alumni of Institute and Alma Mater. We invite the alumni for career guidance and counselling to undergraduate and postgraduate students through Alumni Talk Series. Alumni Meets are organized every year to re-establish the connection with our alumni and making a stronger bond with the Institute. The Alumni Association of NITUK is connecting the alumni with NITUK, emotionally, financially and provides a platform of personal growth.

•Between the Alumni themselves: Share knowledge, experiences, views and opportunities

•Between Alumni and Faculty: Networking with Industry, Supplement their teaching resources with guest lectures, seminars and collaborative research efforts. 

• Between Alumni and Students: Mentoring, internships and placement opportunities, sponsorships, prizes and prestigious  nomination awards.

• Between Alumni and Institution: Contribute in institution’s efforts towards social causes and building infrastructure.

Industry-Institute Interaction

 The major objective of Industry-institute interaction is to develop a bond between Industry and Academia for mutual benefit and growth of industries as well as institutions. To prepare engineering graduates ready for the jobs in industries, it is necessary to know the requirements of the industries through industry-institute interaction. The Industry-Institute Interaction provides the platform to understand the best engineering practices, latest technological advancement, promote industrial experts to patriciate in curriculum design and exposure to engineering students for industrial atmosphere. To promote Industry-Institute Interaction, following objectives are being undertaken: 

•       Memorandum of Understanding between the Institute and Industries. 

•       Invite Industries to establish chair professor at NIT, Uttarakhand. 

•       Participation of Industry experts in curriculum design. 

•       Organizing Workshops, Conferences and training program with Joint collaboration with Industries. 

•       B.Tech. Projects and M.Tech. dissertation work in industries under joint guidance of the faculty and experts from the  Industry. 

•       Consultancy and Industrial testing by the faculty to Industry. 

•       Internship and Practical training of students in Industries. 

•       Encourage industry executives and practicing engineers to visit the institution for delivering expert lectures on best engineering practices,       technological advancement and experiences. 

•       Visit of Industry experts and engineers to the Institute for seeing research work and laboratories. 

•       Invite Industries to establish research laboratories in emerging area of technology for mutual benefits. 

•       Invite Industry Professionals to visit campus and deliver 1, 2 & 3 credit course, which are typically 10-12, 20-24 or 32-36 hours of engagement of students which can be done on weekends depending upon Availability. 

•       Invite the Industries to establish centers of advance technologies for professional of industries leading to PG/Ph.D. Program for their Employees. 

•       Invite Industry to engage students of NIT Uttarakhand as intern for 2 to 6 months on job training.

 

Recreational/Extra Curricular activities

Student Activities

The Student Welfare Section is one of the Institute's most active and vibrant Sections. The Dean of Student Welfare handles all activities and issues related to student life, such as hostel management, in association with the Chief Warden, Council of Student Activities (CSA), and Sports Section.

Cultural Club: This club aims to create a platform where students can come together to share their unique traditions, customs, music, dance, and art forms. Through various events, performances, and workshops, the Cultural Club encourages members to explore different cultural perspectives, foster understanding, and promote inclusivity.

Literary Club: The Literary Club is motivated by a shared love for literature and the written word. Its purpose is to provide a space where students can explore and discuss various forms of literature, including novels, poetry, short stories, and plays.

Technical Club: The club aims to enhance members' technical skills, encourage collaboration, and inspire creativity in fields such as robotics, coding, electronics, and design. By fostering a spirit of curiosity and exploration, the Technical Club empowers students to develop practical problem-solving abilities and prepares them for future careers in STEM.

Film and Photography Club: The Photography Club is motivated by a shared enthusiasm for capturing and appreciating the world's beauty through the lens. Its primary aim is to provide a space where students can learn and develop their photography skills, exchange ideas, and explore various aspects of photography, including composition, lighting, editing, and storytelling.

Adventure Activities and Community Service Club: The Institute's unique feature is its adventure sports program, which organizes various adventurous activities for students and staff throughout the year with the help of professional experts, such as river rafting, skiing, camping, cycling, tracking, and mountaineering.

National Service Scheme (NSS)

National Institute of Technology Uttarakhand started the NSS unit in the session 2022-23. The NSS aims to provide young students with hands-on experience in delivering community service. The Institute's NSS team allows the students to actively contribute their services for the cause of the community and the nation, thus helping them develop their personalities. 

Student’s Grievance Redressal Cell

The Institute has a Student Grievance Redressal Cell to address students' grievances. It consists of a Coordinator, a Warden (Boys), a Warden (Girls), four student representatives, and institute counsellors as members who work under the guidance and chairmanship of the Dean (SW). 

Along with academic excellence, NIT Uttarakhand promotes extra-curricular activities to ensure all-round development of personality. _The motto of Section is “Sports for all and all for Sports”. The Student Activity and Sports Section is the central hub for student activities.  The SAS Section encourages the students, staff and faculty members to regularly participate in various Games and Sports, Fit India Movement events, Yuva Tourism events, Adventurous activity and Yoga & meditation. In spite of being a new NIT, the Institute has been   successful in developing a firm foundation in the field of sports. Sports develop and encourage the spirit of healthy competition. Sports have the power to provide a universal framework for learning values, thus contributing to the development of soft skills needed for responsible citizenship.

Sports Facility: Institution provides good sports facilities, equipment, and recreational and competitive sports programmes to the students and staff members of the institute and encourages them to take part in games & sports to keep themselves fit and inculcate a spirit of sportsmanship amongst themselves & also encouraging student to participate in various sports competitions. Institution provides Indoor and Outdoor Sports facilities for various Games and Sports like Football, Cricket, Badminton, Table-Tennis, Volleyball, Basketball, Carom, Kabaddi, Kho-Kho, Chess, Gym and much more. The Sports arena is near the boy’s hostel and is also with well-equipped facilities. Students have opportunity to play at any time to have fun.

Events/ Tournaments:  The SAS Section regularly organize the various  Intra NIT & Open tournaments in various  Games and Sports, adventurous activities like tracking, mountaineering, ice tracking , Yoga & meditation training programs, various fit India activity, various coaching throughout the year. SAS Section organizes a sports coaching camp in various games. The Aim of the coaching camps is to impart skilled based training in various games & sports to the players. As a result the skills based coaching helps to develop hidden potential of the students and their skills getting the right direction.

Participation Opportunity: The SAS Section encourages the students, staff and faculty members to regularly participate in various Games and Sports in All India Inter-NIT & Intra NIT Tournament, open tournaments and adventurous activities throughout the year. Besides participating in the Inter NIT Sports Tournament, the institute teams also play practice matches with local Clubs and Institutes teams. This helps in increasing the skill level of players and at the same time provides them with an opportunity to interact with local players.

Adventurous Activities: Uttarakhand lies in the Himalayan range with a lot of opportunity for Adventurous activities. Adventure sports is the unique feature of the Institute and various adventurous activities like river rafting, skiing, camping, cycling, tracking and mountaineering are organized by the institute throughout the year with help of professional experts to the students and staffs. These Sports are not only a source of ‘thrill and joy’ to participants, but also provide a valuable opportunity for developing such traits so as to prepare them for facing challenges against all odds confidently and efficiently. Adventurous activities provide a valuable opportunity to expose the youth to nature, kindling the spirit of adventure; develop personality, character, courage and confidence, to prepare the students for facing challenges against all odds confidently and efficiently.

Location and Accessibility

NIT Uttarakhand, Srinagar campus is located at Srinagar (Garhwal). Srinagar is 105 Km from Rishikesh on NH-58 heading towards Badrinath (193 Km from Srinagar). The nearest railway stations are Rishikesh and Haridwar. Nearest Airport is situated at Jolly Grant, Dehradun which is 125 Km away from Srinagar. Taxis/Buses are available from Airport as well as the railway station for Srinagar. It takes 2-3 hours to reach Srinagar from Rishikesh.
