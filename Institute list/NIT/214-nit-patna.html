About the Institute

About NIT, Patna

National Institute of Technology (NIT) Patna is created by the Ministry of Education, Govt. of India rechristening the erstwhile Bihar College of Engineering Patna in 2004. It is imparting high quality education and values at UG (B.Tech, B.Tech + M.Tech (Dual Degree), B. Arch), PG (M.Tech, MURP) & Ph. D programmes through its experienced faculty and well-equipped laboratories.

At present the Institute is offering UG programme in 10 disciplines, viz., Architecture & Planning, Civil Engineering, Computer Science & Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Electronics & Communication Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Department of Applied Physics and  Materials Engineering, Department of Chemical Science and Technology, Department of Mathematics and Computing Technology, Department of Mechatronics and Automation Engineering, postgraduate in Sixteen disciplines and Ph D in all the Eleven Departments. Institute currently enrols ~3500 undergraduate, ~400 post graduate and ~ 1500 research scholars.

NIT Patna is committed for academic excellence, human values and society upliftment. In short span of time, we have considerably improved our academic and research standards, innovation and entrepreneurship activities, visible through our Institute NIRF ranking which we could improve from 188 to 55.  Based on our performance and proactive activities, we could able to setup ISRO Regional Academic Center for Space, and Telecom Center of Excellence for training & research by Telecom Sector Skill Council, to name a few.

National Institute of Technology Patna aims at setting out very high education standards and holds long record of academic excellence. The pedagogical aspects have been formulated to suit not only the needs of the contemporary industrial requirements but also to develop human potential to its fullest extent in a range of professions. Extra-curricular activities are planed through games and sports, cultural programmes and NSS activities. Cultural activities provide a platform to know about the culture of various states and regions of the country and opportunity for national integration.

 About NIT Patna Bihta Campus:

Over the last 17 years, NIT Patna has developed a good infrastructure to cater its teaching, learning, research and innovation needs. However, the present campus at Ashok Rajpath has only ~ 33 Acres of space, which is now fully saturated restricting any further growth and extension of activities and facilities. In year 2016 Government of Bihar has provided 125 Acres of Land at Bihta for the development of its Additional Campus. A master plan has been developed for ~ 6500 students in phased manner. Min. of Education, Govt of India has released Rs 499.21 Crore fund through HEFA for the development of NIT Patna Bihta campus with a total built-up area of ~ 1,12,000 Sq mt. NBCC has been selected as PMC and M/s Ahluwalia Contract as its builder. The project is of 21 month duration and started on 2nd August 2022.

There is a continuous, considerable all around growth and progress of the Institute which is amply reflected through Institute NIRF ranking. To mention specifically, it was 182 in the year 2018 , improved to 134, 92 and 72 in the year 2019, 2020 and 2021, respectively. In the year 2022 NIRF ranking we could reach upto 63rd position and in the year 2023 NIRF rank was 56. As per this year ranking (2024) we have been placed at 55.  Now, we are unable to expand our activities further due to lack of space in the present campus, which restricts us to create new infrastructures needed for purposes like, starting of new thrust area courses, setting-up Centre of Excellences, Entrepreneurship and Start-up cells, etc.

The infrastructure created in the NIT Patna additional campus will remove these bottle neck and our multi-dimensional activities will get further strengthened. The development of Bihta Campus will provide steep jump in scaling our activities and will certainly provide a further quantum jump in our NIRF Ranking also.

Present Institute students’ strength (UG, PG, and Ph.D.) is ~ 4000; ~1500 students will remain at the present campus and ~2500 students will be housed at Bihta campus. Civil Engineering and Architecture & Planning Departments besides first year B. Tech program will be operated from the present campus. The remaining other Departments, like, Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Electronics & Communication Engineering, Computer Science and Engineering, Department of Applied Physics and  Materials Engineering, Department of Chemical Science and Technology, Department of Mathematics and Computing Technology, Department of Mechatronics and Automation Engineering and Humanities & Social Science will be moved to Bihta Campus. NIT Bihta campus will be a comprehensive integrated campus with all the needed facilities for teaching, learning, research, development, innovation and entrepreneurial   activities at a place. It will house all the Academic Departments, Administrative block, Data Center, Institute Library, CEP Center, Workshops, Research, Instruments & Innovation Center, Separate Boys and Girls Hostels, Canteen and Messes, Faculty and Staff Quarters, Students Activities Center, Play grounds, etc.

Institute Bihta campus has been planned for incorporating the followings features: i) Campus with a vibrant environment for learning, ‘community’ living and recreation, ii) Compatible with minimum of four star GRIHA rating for Green buildings and site planning; iii) Genetic Heritage Park / Eco Park / Herbal Park/ Central Landscape Green Garden provisions, which will provide a pleasant campus experience to the residents and  students; iv) Campus with Green Corridor - An Informal Pedestrian Spine created in between Forest in a manner that creates a tranquil learning and living environment in harmony with the nature; v) Highly Energy-Efficient Building envelope: With appropriate Sun-path analysis for natural light, solar panels for electric generation, solar water heaters for hostels are proposed; vi) 100% organic waste conversion and on-site use of manure for landscaping; vii) Zero Discharge: 100% treatment of gray water generated by using STP plant and reuse of treated water for flushing and landscaping; besides so many other modern amenities and facilities.

NIT Patna Bihta campus is planned to be developed in two years and will provide state of the Art facilities to its stake holders and a big leap toward Institute growth.  Currently 90% structural work has been completed. 778 STUDENTS have been shifted in session 2024-2025 with all facilities. The Centre of Excellence based upon the need of our have been incorporated with the introduction of new specialisations, such as Material engineering and Mechatonics. Development of NIT Bihta campus will alleviate the Institute restriction in starting newer Departments, Contemporary Courses, Centers of Excellence, PG Programs and other Entrepreneurial activates due to the present space crunch. The presence of IIT Patna, DMI, Software Technology Park, IIM, CIPET and MSME  in its neighborhood will provide additional win-win situation to the all and will prove to be catalyst in the Industrial growth and innovation for Bihar.

Facilities

Facilities

The Institute campus is spread over 36 acres and a picturesque river view with numerous historic buildings presenting a spectacle of architectural delight and natural beauty. It is partially residential in nature providing residential facilities to some of its students as well as to the faculties and staff as per availability. Behind the historic administrative building lies the main academic building, which also houses the Civil Engineering Department and the Information Technology departments. A Central Library building at the entrance of the institute has all possible modern facilities including e-resources. The Electrical and Mechanical Engineering Departments face one another about 200 meters west from the Administrative Building. The Department of Architecture, Electronics & Communication, Computer Science & Engineer and Centre for Water Resources Studies are located in separate buildings 300 meter to the east. The Computer Center building besides civil engineering department has state-of-art facility for computation and communication. Student’s activities center, new Girl’s Hostel, smart class rooms and large lecture theatres with modern equipment’s & amenities have been constructed recently. Several other facilities like, separate medical centre and open auditorium are also available.

The Institute has play ground where many National level football and cricket matches arranged in the past when Patna had no stadium. The extra-curricular activities include facilities for outdoor & indoor games, boating & rowing, open air theatre and availability of the local chapter of SPICMACAY. While banking facility is available in campus, Post-office and shopping centers are in the near vicinity. NIT Patna is the Institutional member of The Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE) and INDEST which are devoted to promotion of quality and standards in technical education.

The Institute runs seven essential services in the campus namely 24 hours Electric Supply, suitable drinking water, sanitation and maintenance of cleanliness, security arrangements, and ambulance service for medical emergency, EPBX and Internet facilities. The library and all departments are under local area network and share the different academic/ student information database including with e-Library through PCs available in various departments, office, library and other offices

Hostel Accommodation

The Institute has five boys’ hostels and one girls’ hostel at present in the existing campus. Each hostel is self-contained with amenities such as a reading room, an indoor games room, a lounge and a dining hall with mess.

Student Life at Institute

Student-Life at the Institute

NIT Patna has five Boys hostels, namely Sone Hostel Block-A, Sone Hostel Block-B, Bagmati Hostel, Kosi Hostel, and Barhmputra Hostel & for girls, Ganga Hostel. The total capacities of Boys hostels are 1500+ and girls hostel is 400+. Electric power supply is for 24 hours in all the hostels. All the hostels have their separate common rooms with amenities like TV, Magazines, Newspapers and employment newspaper and indoor facilities such as Table tennis, carom, and chess. The hostels have ample space for cultural and other activities. Facilities of some outdoor games such as badminton, volleyball and basket-ball are also available within the hostel premises.  The available open spaces provide opportunity for outdoor meetings, cultural programme, quiz, yoga, or any group discussions. Besides above amenities, hostels have very good and spacious kitchen and dining space that caters breakfast, lunch, dinner, tea, snacks etc. to the boarders.  All the hostels also have water cooler. Full day service of sweeper & cleaners are available. The hostels are newly renovated with required furniture and regular maintenance. The ambience is calm to support studying environment for the boarders.  

Every year sports activities are planned for boys and girls students to provide them a healthy environment and to exhibit their talents in these activities. The students also participate in national level games and sports competition organized by various institutes of the country.

The foundation day of NIT Patna is being celebrated on 28th January every year. 

Annual Techno and cultural Fest namely Corona symbolizing creativity, enthusiasm and team spirit among the young generation of present day competitive age was organized. Through these events it is intended to provide a common platform for showcase their talents in various fields.

Training & Placement

TRAINING AND PLACEMENT

The placement season for 2024 pass out batch saw 118 companies coming to the campus for offering jobs to the undergraduate and postgraduate students of NIT Patna. The placement season commenced on 24th July 2023 and the day-one slot was given to both PhonePe and Oracle, both of whom offered a CTC of around 16 lakhs per annum. Corporate giants namely IBM, Sabre, Intuit, Microsoft, Samsung, Accenture, Texas Instruments, Amazon, Adobe among others offered employment to our students. The highest package was Rs 45 lakhs per annum offered by Intuit followed by Atlassian which offered 35 lakhs per annum. Extremely reputed companies like Tata Elxsi, GE Digital, PWC, Blackrock, Deloitte, Morgan Stanley also formed a part of the placement portfolio. One of the highlight of the season was NASDAQ offering a PPO to one of our CSE student.

In the core sector eminent employers like Alstom, Nvidia, L&T ECC, Mahindra & Mahindra, Tata Power, Tata Chemicals, Ashok Leyland,
Wabtech, Reliance Jio, Jindal Steel, Aditya Birla, Adani group, Vedanta and BPCL among others offered employment to our students. The total percentage of job offers was 87 % and the average salary was Rs 11.47 lakhs per annum. Besides this many students had also opted for
higher studies and took up entrepreneurial challenges.

NIT Patna also actively supports its students for obtaining internships in reputed companies. We have designed a special brochure which we send
to all corporates underlining our two month internship program after the sixth semester and a full semester internship program in the eighth
semester of UG students. The institute also has a two semester internship program for PG students. Our students received more than 200 internship offers from big corporates like Amazon, Atlassian, Oracle, PhonePe, Samsung (R&D) among others. The highest internship remuneration was Rs. 1,30,000 per month with an average remuneration of more than Rs. 35000/-. Students of our institure who have a panache for research, also went to reputed institutes like IITs and NITs for their internships.

Location and Accessibility

Existing campus of NIT Patna is situated on the south bank of river Ganges behind Gandhi Ghat, one of the most important reverence places of Patna. The Institute campus is 8 km from the Patna Junction railway station and 15 km from the Jai Prakash Narayan International Airport Patna. The entry to the institute is from Ashok Rajpath and it is about 3 km from Gandhi Maidan.

Additional campus of NIT Patna, Bihta Campus is around 40 KM from Patna city, & around 7 KM from proposed Bihta Airport and around 6 KM from Bihta Railway station. This additional campus is likely to be operational in July 2024 in campus of 125 Acres area at Mauja Sikanderpur, Bihta to accommodate 2500 students.
