About the Institute

The National Institute of Technology Arunachal Pradesh was inaugurated on 18th August, 2010 as a member of a group of ten new NITs. These new NITs were established as center of excellence in technical education to combat the growing need of technological professionals in India as well as in the world. The Institute has been through many trials since its establishment in 2010; but ultimately has triumphed over all challenges and is thriving today with more than 800 students on roll and many distinguished members of faculty as well as a good many numbers of competent administrative personnel. Institute offers, UG, PG and Ph.D programs in CSE, EE, ECE, ME, CE, CHE, and Bio.Tech. NIT Arunachal Pradesh was ranked 200th in 2020 NIRF ranking, progressively increased to 160th in 2021 NIRF ranking, secured 171th in 2022 and secured rank-band 101-150 in 2023  and 2024 issued by Ministry of Education Govt. of India. This year, 2025-26, only six UG courses  are being offered namely, BT (Biotechnology), CS (Computer Science & Engineering), CE (Civil Engineering), EE (Electrical Engineering), EC (Electronics & Communication Engineering), ME (Mechanical Engineering).

Facilities

General Facilities

The facilities and services that NITAP renders to the students, faculty and staff members make it stand among the top level institutes. The amenities provided to the students are Banking service, Post Office, Medical facilities, Shopping Complex, Canteen, Guest House for Institute invitees and Multi-Gym etc. However, a resort near to the Jote campus is available to accommodate any visitors. In addition to so, good hotels are available in Itanagar. The facilities available 24 X 7 are water, electricity, internet etc. The institute and hostels are equipped with Indoor-outdoor sports facilities, Internet and LAN facility so as to get relevant information for academic development.

 

Central Library


The Library of the Institute is a knowledge centre. The Central Library of NIT, Arunachal Pradesh was established on August, 2010. The library has a good number of books and journals which are supplemented on regular basis. It has a rich collection of information resources mainly in Engineering, Science and also Management. Reading room facilities are available for thirty students. Xerox and Printing facilities are available for students on payment basis. Online Public Access Catalogue (OPAC) service through Libsys, E-Books and E-Journals access facilities is also available in the Central Library. At present the Central Library hols above 27139 volumes, 4000+ number of titles, 32 number of Journals and more than 26 number of magazines.

Besides the Central Library the Institute also has departmental Libraries catering the need of students and researchers.

Communication skills help the students to find proper placement for which National Institute of Technology, Arunachal Pradesh has latest state of art Language Laboratory with a capacity of 32 students, which make language learning a whole new experience meanwhile help students to improve their English speaking with ease and in the simplest way.

Student Life at Institute

Hostels

The Institute being an Institute of National Importance is fully residential in character. There are three halls of residence for students in total, two for boys (Lohit and Papum hostels) and one for girls (Subansiri hostel).  Internet facility is also provided to all the students in the Institute and hostels with wi-fi connectivity. The hostel are taken care of by the team of wardens, student's representative under the overall control of Chief Warden, beside students have their own presidents for various activities like Mess management, sports managements etc. A team of faculty members pay regular visits to the hostels for improvement and to observe anti ragging measures making the institute a zero ragging.

 

Class Room

Classes are going on in required modern state-of-art classrooms with LCD projectors, smart rooms, video conferencing facilities, digital podium.

 

Seminar Hall

The Institute is having a modern Seminar Hall. Workshops of all streams are organized, providing the students an interface for practical and expertise knowledge. Apart from this, motivational and inspirational lectures of Director are held to flourish every student’s innovative ideas by discussing them. 

Interactive lectures from the faculty members, explore the research, principles and classroom phases by such an effective presentation strategy, are also held at here.

ADDOVEDI (MOTIVATION FOR INNOVATION)

The Festivals of Technology

Addovedi is the annual technical festival of NIT Arunachal Pradesh. It is the extravaganza of technology and an ocean of knowledge. It is organized by the student community of the institute.

 

Annual Cultural Festival (ATULYAM)

“Our Endeavour is to start an odyssey which will inculcate in us the zeal and spirit to embrace the galaxy of brilliance in our midst and will lead us all to laurels of fame and glory”.

Atulyam is the inter-College Annual Cultural Festival of NIT-Arunachal Pradesh. It is one of the important cultural fest in North East as well as in the Country. It has entertainment and fun integrated with awareness and responsibility while creating a holistic and magical environment making this festival grand, extravagant and worthwhile.

Atulyam is the time when students and teachers take a break from the classroom atmosphere and bask in the glory of both Indian and western culture. There are songs, dance, plays and much more. It is the time, when young, old and carefree spirits come together for moments to remember for the whole lifetime. It invites students from all around the country to put their ingenuity and aptitude to test at an unparalleled level of competition. This 3rd edition of Atulyam is epitomized itself as the ultimate platform of participants. Embodying the undying spirit of a student, this festival makes you challenge and ravish the artist in him/her.

Location and Accessibility

Travel Guide

By Rail: The nearest railway station from Jote campus is located at Naharlagun, which is about 30km. Car/Institute bus services are available from Naharlagun Railway Station to the Jote.
By Air: The nearest Airport is Holongi (Itanagar), which is around 40 km from the Institute and nearest helipad is at Naharlagun. Daily helicopter services are available between Guwahati and Naharlagun except on Sundays. The flight services for Holongi (Itanagar) Airport  is available on everyday except Wednesday.
By Road: Itanagar is at a distance of 360 km from Guwahati. Day and Night bus services are available from Guwahati to Itanagar from where taxi/Institute bus can be availed to reach Jote.
