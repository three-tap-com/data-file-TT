About the Institute


Located in Hamirpur district of Himachal Pradesh, NIT Hamirpur enjoys a really scenic environment and pleasant weather. Established in the year 1986, as REC Hamirpur, NIT Hamirpur has been declared as the Institute of National Importance under the Act of Parliament, 2007. The Institute enjoys complete autonomy in academic affairs and it is managed by an autonomous Board of Governors consisting of nominees of the Central and State Governments, Industry and other Educational Institutions/Universities. The Institute is fully financed by Ministry of Education, Government of India since April 2003. In conformity with the policy of Government of India, 50% seats are reserved for students from Himachal Pradesh and remaining 50% are earmarked for students from other states. Foreign Students are admitted under various schemes of Government of India like DASA, ICCR and MEA.

The Institute campus is located at Hamirpur District of Himachal Pradesh. The campus is located in the picturesque surrounding and facing snow clad Dhauladhar Mountain ranges. It is surrounded by lush green pine trees. The campus is well laid with roads, electricity installations, water supply, underground drainage, etc. The place has healthy climate with moderate temperature normally ranging from 03 degree Celsius (minimum) to 40 degree Celsius (maximum) with an altitude of 900 meters.

The Institute provides undergraduate and postgraduate education in engineering fostering the spirit of national integration among the students, a close interaction with industry and a strong emphasis on basic and applied research. The Institute has made tremendous strides in the areas of curriculum development, laboratory & infrastructure development and research. The Institute offers UG, PG and PhD programs in various disciplines of Engineering/Architecture. The institute also offers PG and PhD programs in the field of Science and Management.

Student Life at Institute

Student life at NIT Hamirpur is a vibrant blend of academics, cultural activities, sports, and personal development—all set against the scenic backdrop of the majestic Dhauladhar mountain ranges. The natural beauty and peaceful environment contribute to an enriching and inspiring experience, offering students a balanced life between studies and recreation.

Campus Life and Environment

The 320-acre campus is not only picturesque but also well-equipped with modern infrastructure. Students enjoy a serene atmosphere ideal for study, introspection, and creativity. The clean air, lush pine forests, and moderate climate (ranging from 3°C in winters to 40°C in summers) make outdoor activities and day-to-day living comfortable and refreshing.

Hostel Life

  • Separate hostels for boys and girls are available on campus, each with essential amenities such as Wi-Fi, common rooms, recreational spaces, and mess facilities.

  • The hostels foster a strong sense of community, encouraging students from diverse regions and backgrounds to bond over shared experiences.

  • Night-time discussions, group study sessions, and festive celebrations within hostels form a memorable part of student life.

Sports and Fitness

  • A full-fledged sports complex with courts and fields for basketball, volleyball, tennis, cricket, football, and athletics supports an active lifestyle.

  • Indoor sports facilities include badminton, table tennis, and a gymnasium.

  • Inter-hostel and inter-departmental tournaments foster a healthy competitive spirit and team bonding.

Cultural and Club Activities

Students are encouraged to explore their passions and talents outside the classroom through:

  • Student-run clubs and societies in fields like music, dance, drama, photography, coding, robotics, entrepreneurship, and literature.

  • Annual cultural and technical fests such as Hill’ffair (cultural festival) and NIMBUS (technical fest) bring together students from all over India, showcasing innovation, talent, and spirit.

  • Open Air Theatre and Student Activity Centre serve as platforms for performances, workshops, guest lectures, and large gatherings.

Academic and Departmental Societies

  • Each department has its own technical and academic societies, which conduct workshops, coding events, paper presentations, and seminars.

  • These platforms offer students a chance to sharpen their professional skills, network with peers and industry professionals, and prepare for competitive opportunities.

Wellness and Support

  • The campus has medical facilities, counseling services, and access to mentors and faculty who support students academically and emotionally.

  • With a focus on holistic development, students are guided to manage stress, explore careers, and find balance in their college life.

Connectivity and Accessibility

  • Despite its hill-town location, the campus is well connected by road to cities like Chandigarh, Delhi, and Shimla.

  • The peaceful isolation from the hustle of urban centres makes it a unique and distraction-free environment for study and personal growth.

Overall, student life at NIT Hamirpur is not just about lectures and labs—it’s about discovery, creativity, resilience, and lifelong friendships forged amidst the mountains.

Location and Accessibility

The campus is situated at Anu in Hamirpur district of Himachal Pradesh and is 4 Kms from main inter state bus stand of Hamirpur on Hamirpur – Awah Devi road. The city of Hamirpur is well connected with the rest of the country by road. The nearest broad gauge rail head is at Una (Himachal Pradesh) which is 80 Kms from Hamirpur. The nearest airport is at Dharamshala (Gaggal, district Kangra) which is about 75 Kms from Hamirpur.
