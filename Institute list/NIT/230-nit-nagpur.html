About the Institute

_Brief Information about Institute

_Visvesvaraya National Institute of Technology (VNIT), Nagpur is one of the 31
National Institutes of Technology in India and an Institute of National Importance
under the National Institutes of Technology Act, 2007. Established in 1960 as one of
the first six Regional Engineering Colleges, VNIT has grown into a premier technical
institution, offering high-quality education, research, and innovation.
The institute offers 9 undergraduate programs (8 B. Tech. and 1 B. Arch.) and 24
postgraduate programs (21 M. Tech. and 3 M. Sc.), along with Ph.D. programs
across various engineering and science disciplines. Admissions are through
national-level tests: JEE, GATE (M. Tech.), and JAM (M. Sc.). Currently, VNIT has
about 3,750 undergraduate students, 716 postgraduate students, and 694 Ph.D.
scholars.
VNIT is actively engaged in cutting-edge research and development, supported by
government and private agencies, and provides consultancy and testing services
that contribute significantly to its growth. Recent initiatives include the launch of an
interdisciplinary ”M. Tech. in Biomedical Engineering”, curriculum revision in line with
NEP guidelines, and expansion of infrastructure such as advanced labs, a library,
mega-mess, hostels, health centre, and a classroom complex.
The campus spans 215 acres, featuring a 1 MW solar power plant and rooftop solar
panels on 14 buildings, reducing the carbon footprint by 808 tons annually. VNIT
hosts a Centre of Excellence with Siemens India on Industry 4.0 and ISRO’s Space
Technology Incubation Centre (STIC).
The institute’s faculty and students are committed to societal impact, as
demonstrated by innovations during the COVID-19 pandemic. As per NIRF 2024,
VNIT ranks 39 th in Engineering and 10 th in Architecture in India.

Training & Placement

Training and Placement

The Training and Placement Department of VNIT Nagpur aims to provide students
with a platform to apply their technical skills and gain valuable experience by working
in leading industries. It also serves as an interface between the institute and companies
seeking talented graduates and postgraduates across various disciplines. The
department offers essential guidance to students for career planning, skill
enhancement, and personality development.
TRAININGS
The Training and Placement Department conducts rigorous training and assessments
for students appearing for campus placements. These trainings cover key areas such
as programming languages, data structures, algorithms, domain knowledge,
professional English communication, and aptitude. Mock interviews in both HR and
technical domains are organized regularly to help students prepare effectively. In
addition, a series of lectures by corporate leaders are arranged to provide insights into
industry expectations from young engineers. The department also conducts
orientation programs for placements, and career guidance seminars focused on higher
education opportunities in India and abroad, enabling students to make informed
career choices.
INTERNSHIPS
Companies offer summer internships to B. Tech. (2nd and 3rd year) and M. Tech. (1st
year) students, providing them with the opportunity to gain hands-on experience on
technologies they learn on campus. Provision for semester-long internships has been
introduced. These internships enhance students’ readiness for professional roles and
often lead to pre-placement offers (PPOs) based on performance.
• Note:
o B. Tech. students can undertake an 8–10 weeks summer internship
o M. Tech. students can opt for a year-long internship during their final
year.

The T&P Department also facilitates applications for internships at PSUs by
arranging NOCs and necessary documentation.

_ _Industry and Alumni Relations

Industry Institute Interaction (III) Cell
The Industry-Institute Interaction (III) Cell at VNIT, established in 1989, promotes
strong collaboration between the institute and industry. It works with academic
departments to design programs aligned with industry needs, create opportunities for
skill upgradation of professionals, and coordinate joint R&D activities.
With evolving economic policies and increasing global competition, industries rely
more on institutes like VNIT for skilled manpower and R&D support. The III Cell aims
to foster a symbiotic relationship, building mutual trust and encouraging industry
involvement at various stages of development.
In recent years, VNIT has signed MoUs with several national and international
organizations, include:
• National Disaster Management Authority (New Delhi)
• All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS, Nagpur)
• Persistent Systems Ltd. (Nagpur)
• Windsun Renewable Pvt. Ltd. (Nagpur)
• Minex Metallurgical Co. Ltd. (Mumbai)

The III Cell also organizes lectures by industry experts—covering at least 20% of the
core curriculum—and facilitates student internships to enhance practical skills and
employability.
Alumni Association
VNIT Nagpur has a vibrant Alumni Association (VNITAA), uniting distinguished
alumni across government, private, and global sectors to promote professional

networking, mentorship, and strong ties with current students. The association
supports VNIT through scholarships, mentorship, workshops, guest lectures,
internships, and entrepreneurship initiatives.
VNITAA has active chapters in major Indian cities and the USA, and has funded
infrastructure projects worth ₹3.5 crores in the past 1.5 years.
Registered as a non-profit under Section 25 of the Indian Companies Act, VNITAA
holds 80G tax exemption.
