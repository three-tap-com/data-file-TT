About the Institute

The National Institute of Technology, Durgapur (formerly Regional Engineering College, Durgapur), was established by an Act of Parliament in 1960 as one of the eight such Institutes aimed to function as a pace setter for engineering education in the country and to foster national integration. It is a fully-funded premier Technological Institution of the Government of India and is administered by an autonomous Board of Governors. The Institute awards B. Tech, B. Tech and M. Tech (Dual Degree), Integrated M. Sc., M.Sc., M.B.A., M. Tech., MSW and PhD degrees to students after their successful completion of the specified courses. The Institute imparts education in the disciplines of Biotechnology, Chemical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Computer Science and Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Metallurgical and Materials Engineering, Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Earth & Environmental Sciences, Humanities and Social Sciences and Management Studies. As decided by the Ministry of Education (MoE), Government of India, the procedure for selection of candidates for admission to the Bachelor Degree Courses in Technology in National Institute of Technology Durgapur and in other NITs is on the basis of JEE (Main) and the same is executed through JoSAA under the guidance from MoE, GOI. In addition to the normal intake, foreign students are admitted through DASA and by the nomination of the Ministry of External Affairs (MEA), Government of India, and the Indian Council for Cultural Relations (ICCR), Government of India and Study in India Program.

The NIRF Rank of the Institute is 44 in Engineering Category in the year 2024, 10th among all NITs.

Location and Accessibility

The Institute is located about 160 KMs north-west of Kolkata on the Howrah-Delhi main railway route and overlooking the National Highway No. 2 (the great Grand- Trunk Road). The nearest Railway station is Durgapur (DGR) 10km away and Asansol (ASN) 35 km away. The nearest airport is Kaji Najrul Islam Airport (RDP) is 20 km away and NSC Bose International Airport Kolkata (CCU) is 180km away. The Institute spreads over an area of 187 acres of land.
