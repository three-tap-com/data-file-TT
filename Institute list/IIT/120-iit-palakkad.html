About the Institute

History

The Indian Institute of Technology Palakkad (IIT Palakkad) was established in 2015 as an institute of national importance by the Government of India under the Institutes of Technology Act, 1961. IIT Palakkad offers B-Tech programs in five engineering disciplines, M.Tech. programs in ten specializations (Geotechnical Engineering, Manufacturing and Materials Engineering, Computing and Mathematics, Data Science, Power Electronics and Power Systems, System-on-Chip Design, Design & Automation, Thermofluids Engineering, Structural Engineering, Water Resources Engineering), M.Sc. programs in three science disciplines (Chemistry, Physics, Mathematics), an M.S. (by Research) program in Engineering, and Ph.D. programs in Engineering, Science, and Humanities & Social Sciences.

Campus

IIT Palakkad operates in its permanent campus with world-class sustainable green buildings on 500 acres of land bordering the picturesque Sahya Mountain range adjacent to the Coimbatore-Kanyakumari national highway. The campus is located at Kanjikode, which is about 7 km from Palakkad town on the Palakkad-Coimbatore highway. The campus has well-equipped laboratories, classrooms and computing facilities, modern hostels for girls and boys, and facilities for sports, fitness and cultural activities. Additional buildings and facilities are presently being developed.

Academic Structure

B.Tech. program

IIT Palakkad has a flexible credit-based curriculum for its programs. Each academic year has two main semesters. The monsoon semester runs from July to December and the winter semester runs from January to May. The B.Tech program is of 8 semesters duration. The dynamic credit-based curriculum allows students to chalk out their graduation plans by choosing from diverse options to gain wider expertise. This includes taking courses outside their professional major, more open electives, options for minor, double major degrees, B.Tech-M.Tech dual degree and B.Tech Honours by Research options to gain breadth and depth. Highlights of the UG curriculum can be found here. 

Every discipline has a curriculum consisting of core and elective courses that are carefully chosen to give a broad grip on the fundamentals of engineering and an in-depth understanding of the respective discipline. Every curriculum has a considerable number of elective courses under different pools. The students have the flexibility to choose the elective courses based on their interests and aspirations. There is also a pool of Humanities, Social Science and other general courses which help the all-round development of the students.

There are plenty of opportunities for students to do various projects throughout the program. The projects are intended to be student-driven, can be hands-on, and product development oriented, to encourage start-up and entrepreneurship activities. Alternatively, research-driven projects can also be taken up for those who want to start research early on. The main aim is to allow students to steer their careers in their chosen paths. 

IIT Palakkad has a grading policy based on continuous assessment. Grades are awarded on a 10-point scale, and the academic performance is quantified by the Grade Point Average (GPA) and the Cumulative Grade Point Average (CGPA).

Rules For Change Branch

All the B.Tech. programs have the same curriculum in the first year. IIT Palakkad allows a selected number of students category-wise to change their branch based on their academic performance in the first year. The change comes into effect at the end of their first year. The number of students who can make a branch change is limited so that the final strength in any branch does not deviate by much.

Facilities

IIT Palakkad has a well-stocked central library. Apart from text and reference books, the library has a collection of magazines and newspapers, and provision to access online material. The central library is functional from 9 am to 11 pm on weekdays and from 9 am to 7 pm on weekends. The institute also has several well-equipped computer labs that can be accessed round the clock, where access to e-books and course materials is provided through the learning management system Moodle. The institute has several classrooms and an auditorium equipped with Wi-Fi enabled state-of-the-art multimedia facilities. All the student laboratories are well established and have the latest equipment. In addition, there is an innovation centre (Csquare) which is well-equipped for the fabrication of any prototype, proof-of concept designs or project work in soft and hard materials. Prominent equipment includes 3D printer, computer numerical controlled (CNC) plasma, router, hot wire and laser cutters for working with metal, thermocol, wood, and acrylic. These facilities are extensively used by students and faculty for various courses and research projects.

IIT Palakkad has set up the Central Instrumentation Facility (CIF), Central Micro-Nano Fabrication Facility (CMFF), Central Facility for Materials and Manufacturing Engineering (CFMM), and ‘Chandra’ the High-Performance Computing Cluster with the primary objective of supporting the research activities of its faculty, students, Ph.D. scholars and Postdoctoral researchers. The facilities house a wide range of sophisticated analytical and fabrication equipment available to the research community. IIT Palakkad has also set up a real-time air quality and weather monitoring station that continuously monitors and reports real-time data on air pollutants and meteorological parameters. The facilities are envisaged to nourish collaborative research between IIT Palakkad and other academic and industrial organizations. 

IIT Palakkad is a fully residential campus, with separate hostels for girls and boys. The Institute has a total of 5 hostels with all essential facilities. The student-run mess operates at two locations within the campus. In-addition, there is an amenity center which caters to day-to-day needs of the students, staff and faculty. Hostel amenities include solar heaters for hot water, spacious dining hall, well-equipped recreational area, indoor games area, fitness centre, RO-based drinking water system, and heavy-duty washing machines in each of the hostels. Other amenities include in-house facilities for playing football, volleyball, basketball, cricket, badminton, and table tennis. There are professional coaches for athletics, football, volleyball and cricket. The common space in the hostel is Wi-Fi enabled, and the rooms have either Wi-Fi enabled or have LAN connectivity. Each hostel has a common room with a television, newspapers and selected magazines for recreation. There are guest rooms for visitors to the Institute faculty, staff and students.

Student Life at Institute

The proximity of hostels and academic facilities and the general ambience of the campus is conducive to vibrant student life. All facilities required for the students are available within the safe confines of the campus itself. In addition, Palakkad town is well known for its cultural heritage, and there are several cultural events throughout the year. There are daily shuttle services between the campus and Palakkad town.  

Student Wellness

A comprehensive medical insurance scheme covers all students for a nominal yearly subscription. IIT Palakkad has MoU with several hospitals in Palakkad for cashless medical care. Students can also avail the facilities of a medical clinic in Nila campus.

Institute Counselling Service (Mitra) has been set up in order to ensure that the students receive help when they face social/emotional issues that require a professional approach. The team comprises three experienced counsellors—two female and one male—who are qualified Clinical Psychologists and available to support students as needed. The Counsellors are available on both campuses: Malhar Hostel (B002) and Bageshri 103A & B. Additionally, Amaha, an online mental health platform, offers therapy and self-care tools to students 24/7. Trained student volunteers on campus also provide peer support.

Financial Assistance

Several scholarships are available to students and are based on the student's financial status and/or academic performance. Merit-Cum-Means scholarships, Scholarships for SC/ST students and students with disability are available to the students of IIT Palakkad, as per the Government of India norms. The Government of India also offers various scholarship schemes through the National Scholarship Portal. In addition, the institute has signed an MoU with the Shraman Foundation to support students from poor socioeconomic backgrounds offering financial assistance to them in addition to the institute scholarships. For more details, please visit the Institute  Scholarships and Financial Assistance webpage.

Training & Placement

Career Development Centre

The Career Development Center (CDC) of IIT Palakkad employs significant efforts to refine the capabilities, personality, and work readiness of students with the help of placement training and career preparation workshops. To facilitate better career opportunities, CDC constantly engages with industry through internships, industry visits, Industry-Academia conclaves, and also by hosting industry experts on campus. A combination of rigorous yet sufficiently flexible curriculum prepares the students for the challenges in a competitive industrial environment. The center is functional under a Faculty In-Charge and the Training and Placement Officer (TPO) dedicated to the endeavor of grooming the students.

International Relations

At IIT Palakkad, faculty members and students have been collaborating with universities globally since its inception. IIT Palakkad officially set up an Office of International Relations in 2021 (https://ir.iitpkd.ac.in). This office oversees and coordinates the international activities on campus, like hosting international delegations, students and researchers, initiating research collaborations and formalizing partnerships. We engage with embassies/consulates and government agencies like Educational Consultants India Limited (EdCIL) to initiate educational and cultural connections and provide opportunities for foreign students to pursue their studies at IIT Palakkad.  We also explore, collate, create and coordinate opportunities for our students to travel abroad to partner universities and benefit from the exposure to different academic, research, and cultural environments. At the international level, collaborations are ongoing with institutions across various countries, including US, Japan, Australia, Germany, Norway, France, Taiwan, Ethiopia etc.

Alumni

IIT Palakkad has graduated six batches of undergraduate and postgraduate students as of 2024. The Alumni Cell of IIT Palakkad was founded in July 2019, and we have about 1200 registered alumni. Even with such a relatively small strength, our alumni are already spread across the globe in various capacities. Academically, our alumni are in universities such as the University of California San Diego, Harvard Medical School, Georgia Institute of Technology, Columbia University, University of Southern California, University of Texas at Austin, University of Zurich, Northwestern University, Pennsylvania State University, Arizona State University, University of California at Los Angeles, HEC Paris, Texas A & M University, Virginia Tech University, Portland State University, University of south Florida, Nanyang Technological University, The University of Texas at Dallas and IIMs; as corporate professionals, our alumni are placed in industries such as Google, Apple, Amazon, Mathworks, VMware, Accenture Japan, Bosch, Hitachi, AirAsia, Arista Networks, OYO, PayTM, TCS, TRAI, ITI, Zomato, Swiggy etc. IIT Palakkad has an active Alumni Cell that constantly engages the Institute and the alumni community in various activities and interactions. The Alumni Cell has also established the first of its kind - undergraduate research and innovations award.

The Institute recognizes alumni who have excelled in their careers and brought pride to the Institute through their achievements.

Location and Accessibility

IIT Palakkad is well connected through trains and busses to Palakkad town and Coimbatore city for shopping and recreational facilities. For the convenience of students, the institute also arranges frequent transport services.

The closest major airport to IIT Palakkad is in Coimbatore, located about 50 km from the campus. IIT Palakkad is also well connected by the railway network and can be easily accessed from Palakkad Junction and Coimbatore Junction railway stations, which are about 15 km and 40 km, respectively. Both these railway stations are well connected with trains from all over India. The nearest local train station is in Kanjikode, located about 4 km from the permanent campus. The nearest bus stand is the Palakkad Municipal Bus Stand at Palakkad. It is well connected to major cities in Kerala and Tamil Nadu through highways.
