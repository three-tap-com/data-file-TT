About the Institute

Indian Institute of Technology Dharwad  is one of the third Generation (3-G) IITs, which were established by the Ministry of Education (MoE), Government of India (GoI). It was started in August 2016 under the mentorship of IIT Bombay. Our academic journey began in the Academic Year (AY) 2016-17, with the establishment of four-year Bachelor of Technology (B. Tech.) programs - Mechanical Engineering (ME), Electrical Engineering (EE) and Computer Science & Engineering (CSE).

Currently, Institute offers four other B. Tech. programs - Engineering Physics (EP), Mathematics & Computing (M&C), Civil & Infrastructure Engineering (C&IE), Chemical & Biochemical Engineering (C&BE) as well as a five-year Bachelor of Science-Master of Science (BS-MS) program, a two-year Master of Technology (M. Tech.) Program and a Master of Technology by research (M. Tech. by research)  Program offered by the three oldest Departments of Mechanical, Materials & Aerospace Engineering (MMAE), Electrical, Electronics & Communication Engineering (EECE) and Computer Science & Engineering (CSE).

The first phase of construction of the 470-acre new IIT Dharwad campus is completed and is located two kilometres from the Pune-Bengaluru National Highway (NH4). The campus design is based on the theme of "Sustainable Green Campus", inspired by historical origins of Hubballi Dharwad. In December 2022, IIT DHARWAD was awarded 5-star Rating under Green Rating for Integrated Habitat Assessment (GRIHA), Large Development (LD) Master Plan. The permanent campus of IIT DHARWAD has many eco-friendly design features such as rain water harvesting, treatment and reuse of Municipal waste water using phytoremediation technology. With the availability of transport service, the campus is easy to reach from any point in Dharwad and all the major facilities are within a 10 km radius from the campus. IIT Dharwad is less than 30 km away from Hubballi, the second-largest city in Karnataka.  

The academic activities of IIT Dharwad are well-tuned to bring out the best in every student. The academics at IIT Dharwad is focused on bringing fun and joy into the learning process by a judicious mix of rigorous classroom coursework backed up by exciting projects where students enjoy experiential learning by hands-on design-build-operate type activities.

Academic Structure

IIT Dharwad follows a semester system. An academic year (July-April) consists of two semesters, each of approximately 16 weeks’ duration. The first semester begins in the last week of July and ends by the last week of November. The second semester starts in the first week of January and ends by the last week of April. In each of the semesters, a student is required to register for the relevant courses listed in the curriculum for that semester.

The Institute follows a credit system. Credits are allotted to various courses depending upon the number of lectures, tutorials and laboratory hours per week. The student’s performance in a course is continuously evaluated throughout the semester and culminates in the award of a Grade on a 10-point scale. Performance in a semester is evaluated in terms of the weighted average of grade points secured in all the courses registered in that semester, which is known as Semester Performance Index (SPI). A Cumulative Performance Index (CPI) is the weighted average of the grade points obtained in all the courses registered by the student since the time of joining the Institute. The B.Tech. programs consist of eight semesters spread over four years. The BS-MS in Interdisciplinary Sciences programme consists of 10 semesters and spread over five years.

Facilities

IIT Dharwad has all the facilities like CLT, CIL and CIF having classrooms, an auditorium, seminar room, library, a computer center, several laboratories, faculty offices and cafeteria. All classrooms and laboratories are network connected and wi-fi facility is provided throughout the campus.  The institute has developed a Master Plan that is green, smart and world-class with state-of-the-art infrastructure complying with the guidelines of GRIHAA, IGBC and NBC.

Hostel facility: The Institute has two hostels now: Hostel -1 & Hostel- 2 which are enough to accommodate both existing students and newly joined students. With a separate dining hall and access to the internet and recreational facilities, the hostel at IIT Dharwad is on par with the best available in any other IIT. 

Sports facilities: IIT Dharwad promotes sports as a vital part of campus life, encouraging fitness, teamwork, and excellence. The Indoor Sports Complex houses 4 Badminton Courts, 2 Squash Courts, 2 Table Tennis Tables, two well-equipped Gyms, and a Boardroom. Outdoor facilities include 2 Volleyball Courts and 1 Basketball Court. A temporary multi-purpose ground also supports Cricket, Football, Basketball, Hockey, and a 400-meter Athletics Track. The campus hosts year-round sports activities such as marathons and tournaments, with significant events including National Sports Day and Inter-Hostel GC Tournaments. The institute has a full-time Sports Officer, a Junior Sports Officer, and part-time coaches for all major games and training to support these initiatives. The sports facilities are open to students, staff, and faculty, ensuring broad participation and fostering a vibrant sporting culture at IIT Dharwad.

Medical facilities: The Institute has Wellness Center which hosts Health and Counselling services. Institute is equipped with round the clock ambulance with an "ICU on wheels" facility under the supervision of skilled paramedic staff. Qualified and experienced doctors visit the campus regularly and are available on call. The students, faculty and staff of IIT Dharwad have access to the general medical facilities available in the assigned local hospitals.
A panel of eminent visiting Doctors provides consultations at the Centre which is a 24x7 facility with Nurses and Ambulance service on duty 24x7. The Psychological Counselling services are also an important component of the Wellness Centre. Apart from online counselling support by a reputed service provider, the counselling unit has two full time counselling psychologists for face to face consultation. A well known psychiatrist is also available on campus by appointment.

Knowledge Resource and Information (Library): The Knowledge Resource Centre collection consists of nearly 6100+ print books in various disciplines. These are arranged subject-wise. It has also acquired a few ISO standards (International Standards Organization) in digital form. The main motto is to build up the KRC collection with information resources up to the PhD level, including advanced texts and reference materials.

IIT Dharwad is a Life Member of the prestigious “Current Science Association” of the “Indian Academy of Sciences” and received the “Current Science” journal. It also receives some national newspapers and light-reading magazines. Very soon, some popular S&T magazines will be at readers’ disposal, which will make young minds aware of recent happenings in the scientific world. Being a part of INFLIBNET e-ShodhSindhu, serious readers can access nearly 9500+ reputed E-Journals (Oxford University, Springer, T&F HSS and Science and Technology, ICE virtual library, IOP, Sage, Wiley, Elsevier ScienceDirect 12 Subject Collection, Elsevier Science Direct Archive Collection, 26 Individual Journals, etc.), Databases (MathSciNet, IEEE Electronic Library, JSTOR, Project Muse, ISID, McGraw Hill Access Engineering, Scopus, etc.), Standards (ISO, IEL, ASTM) and Society Publications (ACS, ACM, AIP, AMS, APS, ASCE, ASME, OSA, RSC, SIAM,  etc.) literature retrospectively.  This collection is the bread-and-butter for the Institute’s research scholars and faculty members to quench their thirst for state-of-the-art knowledge.

Student Life at Institute

The proximity of  hostel and academic building provides for easy access to various activities. The quiet and picturesque location is conducive for study and other activities. City climate is moderate and healthy and average rainfall during the season.

The twin city of Hubballi-Dharwad has a special status at both state and national level. It is culturally very rich and offers opportunities to enjoy classical music (Savai Gandharva, Mallikarjun Mansoor, Gangubai Hangal and Bhimsen Joshi hail from this region), Fine arts and Theatre. The city also houses many renowned educational institutions like IIIT, Karnataka University and University of Agricultural Sciences and many medical and Engineering colleges.

Culture and Sports

At IIT Dharwad, Culture is perceived as a path to individual enrichment, as well as a way of life. The IIT Dharwad campus boasts of a vibrant cultural landscape. The new institution already has a number of student-driven clubs, from Music Club to Photography Club, to Electronics Club, Curiosity Club and Astronomy Club. Apart from academic engagements, students also participate in a number of co-curricular actvities such as NCC, NSS and NSO and extra-curricular activities- such as learning Clay Modelling (through sessions conducted weekly) and making short films. The campus also witnesses a number of competitions in essay-writing, speech, dance and sketching. The diverse demography of the campus ensures that students celebrate different Indian festivals. Additionally, culture is given a big phillip by SPICMACAY events including concerts by top artists and regional conventions full of various workshops to learn different Indian art forms.

Sports is another major component of life at IIT Dharwad. The campus has a well-equipped Gym, full fledged play ground, swimming pool, and several sports activities run all through the year- from cross-country marathons to cricket matches, for students, staff and faculty. We have dedicated full time sports officers. Facilities for indoor games such as Table Tennis are available.

Location and Accessibility

IIT Dharwad is located at about 12 kilometers from the Dharwad city and 14 kilometers from Dharwad railway station. Around 37 kilometers away, Hubballi is a junction and big railway head. The campus is right on the national highway NH4 and is easily accessible by road. The nearest airports to reach IIT Dharwad are at Belgaum (connected to Mumbai, Chennai, Hyderabad etc.) and Hubballi (connected to Bengaluru, Mumbai etc.). Goa airport is about 3 hours by road.
