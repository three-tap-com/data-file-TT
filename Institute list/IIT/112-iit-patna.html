About the Institute

About the Institute:

Indian Institute of Technology Patna came into existence in 2008 by an Act of Parliament along with seven (07) other IITs, then termed as 2nd generation IITs. IIT Patna mission is to achieve "eminence" through vision of "excellence".  Over a period of more than one and half decade, now IIT Patna has created a mark of itself in academics, research, innovations, entrepreneurship and employability of its graduates (B. Tech), postgraduates ( M.Tech./ M Sc.) and Doctorate alumni. The success story of IIT Patna alumni spreads worldwide and has created a niche for themselves, has truly established the IIT Patna vision and mission. The Institute aims to encourage collaborative activities along with leadership skills among students. Students are encouraged to participate in various co-curricular activities and visits to nearby industrial units which help in honing their skills and knowledge. The Institute aims to take students into a world of discovery, innovation and self-reliance which will lead to inclusive development not only of the individual but also of the society at large. IIT Patna is perhaps the 1st IIT to implement National Education Policy (NEP- 2020) and ready to start flexible academic curriculum with skill focused teaching learning from AY 2023-24.

About Patna

Patna is an ancient city and was earlier known as 'Patliputra'. Situated on the banks of the river Ganges, this city has been a centre of knowledge and has attracted visitors from China, Indonesia, Japan, Korea and other nations. The ancient city was home to some of the greatest minds of the time like Aryabhatta who worked on Astronomy, Algebra and trigonometry and introduced the concept of 'zero', Chanakya who is famous for his treatises on Economics and Politics. Modern Patna too does not lag far behind. It has produced illustrious people like Dr. Rajendra Prasad, the first President of independent India and many political figures and jurists of renown. Its rich history is visible in the various buildings which showcase elements of Afghani, Mughal and British architecture. Some of   the historical legends from this region include Lord Gautam Buddha, Lord Mahavir, Guru Govind Singh.

Department of Chemical and Biochemical Engineering

The vision of Chemical and Biochemical Engineering department at IIT Patna is to achieve national and international recognition through the educational and research achievements and the professional service of our faculty, staff and students. Our mission is to provide students with an overall educational experience that prepares our students to become successful professional chemical engineers capable of undertaking both life-long learning and further advanced studies.

The department offers B.Tech., M.Tech., M.Tech. by Research, and Ph.D. programs. These programs aim to produce highly-qualified graduates who are self-motivated, and independent. The graduates are equipped with skills to undertake independent work in private and public sector industries and in research and development programs. Over the last several years, the department has achieved a good placement record for the undergraduate students. Majority of our UG students are placed in reputed industries across the sectors while others opt for higher education both within and outside the nation.

The department has fully developed undergraduate laboratories and modern research facilities. Our departmental library, has a good collection of reference books related to chemical and biochemical engineering. The department regularly organizes events such as conferences, seminars, and workshops on its own and in collaboration with other elite institutions. The department has a good student-faculty ratio and all faculty members are engaged in cutting edge research at international level. The research laboratories include biochemical lab, computational nanoscience lab, process systems engineering lab, energy science lab, separation processes lab, and gas-solid interaction lab. Several industrial and external funded research projects are ongoing in the department. The department has implemented externally funded research projects worth around 3 crore rupees.

Our student club ChESSx regularly organizes technical, cultural, and sports vents. Students often take part in competitions and hackathons across the nation and in international events. Several UG students frequently participate in summer and winter internships at various PSUs, petrochemical industries, and other MNCs.

Tech. degree in Structural Engineering, Geotechnical Engineering, Rock Engineering for Infrastructure Development, Transportation Engineering, Environmental Engineering and Dual Degree (B. Department of Metallurgical and Materials Engineering

The department of metallurgical and materials engineering is dedicated to the rudimentary understanding of materials with emphasis on the basic concepts of structure/property/processing/ performance relations and on applications to a wide variety of engineering fields. The department currently has eight highly accomplished faculty members. The department offers a B. Tech. degree in metallurgical and materials engineering and a M. Tech. degree in materials science and engineering. The postgraduate program is more interdisciplinary in approach and nature. After taking feedback from both academia and industries the syllabi of the respective programs are proposed and designed. The curriculum for these programs has been developed keeping in view the present technological status and future needs and prospects. It provides a strong background and a holistic approach towards conventional materials like metals as well as advanced materials like electronic materials, ceramics, composites, and polymers. The course curriculum is swotted periodically to incorporate new emerging trends in technologies and industrial advances. Apart from the theoretical courses, the course curriculum also has large laboratory/practical work components, internship components, seminars, and B.Tech./M.Tech. projects and dissertations. Furthermore, the students of the department are specifically trained to develop materials for new applications, improve the properties of existing materials, enhance performance, and evaluate ways by which different materials can be used collectively. The department houses world-class research facilities under one roof. Faculty members of the department are actively involved in the execution of strong inter-disciplinary fundamental/applied research encompassing an extensive array of materials including metals, ceramics, polymers, nanomaterials, energy materials, biomaterials, to name a few. The department also has a strong base in coating technology, material synthesis, processing and computational material science. In the past decade, the department has been very successful in receiving grants from various government and industrial funding agencies such as DRDO, DST, ISRO, BRNS, NRB, Tata Steel, DENKA Japan, MRF Tyres, Apollo Tyres, TVS Tyres, Sundaram Brake Linings, Danfoss, Plasmatron Private Limited, Manali Petrochemical Industries Limited, and Carborundum Universal Limited. We have also developed and established strong collaborations with various universities in India and abroad, defense laboratories, and industries. The department has received research grants of more than 18 crores. The department faculty members have published more than 175 papers, and 10 Ph.D. students have graduated from the department. Few of our Ph.D. students are now faculty members in other IITs. The department has established the "center of excellence in wear and corrosion resistance coating", funded by the DST along with the industry partners like Tata Steel Limited, Applied Materials and Associated Plasmatron Private Limited. A national user facility on flash sintering of ceramics is being set up in the department, which is funded by Rishi and Jyotsna Raj center for electrification of materials manufacturing. The first batch of B. Tech. students graduated in 2023. The department's B. Tech., M. Tech. students and Ph.D. scholars have bagged placements in various core companies, MNCs, research laboratories, IITs and public sector units. Some of the B. Tech and M. Tech students have also acquired M.S./ Ph.D. positions in various top-rated universities in India and abroad. The B.Tech. students of the department conducingly run the materials engineering society (MatES), which regularly organizes invited/key-note lectures and technical exhibitions/quiz competitions. The postgraduate students of the department are the in-charge for organizing the department level technical symposium (Dravya) to create a platform for the research scholars to share their research findings. This symposium also aims to encourage discussions related to the current challenges and future opportunities pertaining to metallurgical and materials engineering. The department continues to strive for excellence and realize its vision of becoming a leading department in the country by integrating research, teaching and practice, which holds great benefits, both from student’s and teacher’s perspectives.
